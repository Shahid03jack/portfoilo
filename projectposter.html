Smart Medicine Reminder Bot

An AI-Integrated Healthcare Assistant Designed to Provide Smart Medication Reminders, Schedule Management, and Real-Time Monitoring for Improved Patient Care.

Problem

Many patients, especially elderly individuals and people with busy schedules, often forget to take their medications on time. Missed doses can reduce treatment effectiveness and negatively impact health outcomes. Traditional reminders such as alarms are often ignored or lack proper monitoring capabilities.



Solution

Developed a Smart Medicine Reminder Bot that provides timely medication reminders, tracks scheduled doses, and assists users in maintaining their medication routine. The system combines automation, notifications, and monitoring features to improve adherence and support better healthcare management.



Tech Stack

Arduino, ESP32, RTC Module, LCD Display, Buzzer, Sensors, Embedded C/C++, Mobile Monitoring Interface



Features

  • Automated medicine reminders at scheduled times
  • Live time monitoring and medication tracking
  • Audio and visual alert system
  • User-friendly interface for managing schedules
  • Improved medication adherence and healthcare support


Outcome

Successfully designed and developed a working prototype capable of providing reliable medicine reminders and monitoring medication schedules. The project demonstrates how automation and embedded systems can contribute to better healthcare assistance and daily life management.
