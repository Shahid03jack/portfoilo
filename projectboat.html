Water Cleaning Boat

Designed and Developed a Multi-Feature Water Cleaning Boat with an Integrated Robotic Arm for Waste Collection, Object Handling, and Environmental Monitoring.

Problem

Water bodies such as rivers, lakes, and ponds are increasingly affected by floating waste, plastic debris, and pollution. Traditional cleaning methods are often labor-intensive, time-consuming, and difficult to implement in large or hard-to-reach areas.



Solution

Designed and developed a multi-feature Water Cleaning Boat capable of collecting floating waste while providing robotic assistance for object handling. The system combines mobility, waste collection, and automation features to improve the efficiency of water-cleaning operations.



Tech Stack

Arduino, DC Motors, Motor Driver, Servo Motors, Bluetooth Module, Embedded C/C++, Robotic Arm Mechanism, Wireless Control System



Features

  • Floating waste collection mechanism
  • Integrated robotic arm for object handling
  • Wireless mobile control via Bluetooth
  • Autonomous and manual operation capabilities
  • Eco-friendly solution for water pollution management


Outcome

Successfully developed a working prototype demonstrating the feasibility of automated water-cleaning operations. The project highlights how robotics and automation can contribute to environmental sustainability by reducing human effort and improving waste management efficiency.
